CORE FEATURES

Built around real academic workflows

Academia Connect reduces manual work while keeping every activity, participant and certificate organized.

Activity management

Organizers can create, edit, manage and cancel academic activities from one dashboard.

Registration tracking

Students can register for activities and view all active registrations from their dashboard.

Automatic waitlists

Full activities automatically place students in an ordered waitlist and promote them when seats open.

Schedule conflict warning

Students receive a warning when attempting to register for activities scheduled at the same time.

Attendance management

Organizers can mark participants as attended or absent before issuing completion certificates.

Feedback and ratings

Participants can submit feedback, while organizers can review responses and average ratings.
